Wonder

A Wikipedia reader

I’m on Wikipedia all the time. While the quality of the content can vary, it gives me at least an overview of the topic I’m interested in, and often more. It’s a good research tool. So when I look for an article, I want to find the right one as fast as possible, identify its relevant parts and browse back and forth between articles.

An app called Articles used to fulfil this role nicely on iOS. Its navigation was inspired by Safari with a search bar at the top of the screen, a navigation bar at the bottom and tabs to navigate between articles. Unfortunately, the app hasn’t been updated in years. I never found a good replacement so after a long wait I decided to create one.

Wonder is a Wikipedia reader for iOS 9 focussed on search, readability and navigation. It doesn’t try to guess what you could want to read, but if you have an idea of what to look for, Wonder is a fast and powerful way to research and learn with Wikipedia.

— Jonathan Thiry
