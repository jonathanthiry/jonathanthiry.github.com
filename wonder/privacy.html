Privacy policy

Wonder does not collect any information.

It gets some anonymous analytics and crash reports from Apple if you've allowed them to share it with the developpers. You can read more about Apple's privacy policy here.

Wonder uses Wikipedia's APIs only, you can read Wikimedia's privacy policy here.
